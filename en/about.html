Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2012

MB Architecture and Design was founded in Algiers with a clear vision: to transform architectural dreams into tangible realities. What began as a small design studio has evolved into a licensed engineering office recognized for excellence and innovation.

Over the years, we have completed more than 150 projects ranging from individual villas to commercial complexes. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation to modern technologies, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner for clients seeking professional architectural services, combining creativity with technical precision to deliver projects that exceed expectations.

Leadership

Mounira B

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in architecture and engineering, Mounira B founded MB Architecture and Design with a passion for creating spaces that inspire and endure. Her vision combines aesthetic excellence with practical functionality, ensuring every project meets the highest standards.

As a licensed architect and engineer, Mounira leads a team of dedicated professionals, fostering a culture of innovation, integrity, and client-focused service. Her leadership has been instrumental in establishing MB Architecture as one of Algeria's premier engineering offices.
